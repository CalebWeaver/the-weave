The Process

Thermal Energy Conflux Limit

Perhaps the best place to begin is the Thermal Conflux Limit. This rune takes is the one which limits the amount of energy pulled from the weave to convert to thermal to make the spell generate enough heat for ignition. The Thermal Conflux Limit is denoted by the separators etli and ilte respectively. Do not confuse the two. Interpreted between the two is a simple quantity indicator.

Applying the Thermal Harness

When seeking to harness the power of thermal energy, you must bind the energies with the appropriate shape and size of the harness. A harness must surround the thermal indicator and apply appropriate stretch to allow the necessary expansion but not so much as to simply heat up the air. The Thermal Harness should be an expansion coefficient denoted as a quantity indicator bound by snarb and brans . The expansion coefficient should be marked starting with naliti and must be a ratio to the Thermal Energy Conflux Limit of at least .1. For a focused effect of no more than 5’ in diameter, the ratio should be no more than .3 and for larger effects, a ratio of no more than .5 should be used. Ratios above .5 should be reserved for local climate control or apocalyptic effects.
